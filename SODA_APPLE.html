Privacy Policy for SODA

Effective Date: July 15, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how SODA, a utility app developed by GISODA Co., Ltd., collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. SODA is designed to help you manage daily tasks, time, expenses, and travel efficiently.

By using SODA, you agree to the collection and use of your information as described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

To provide SODA’s features, we collect information necessary to support task management, time tracking, expense reporting, and travel planning. This includes:

  • Personal and Account Information: To set up and manage your account, we collect details like your name, email, and account credentials you provide.
  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect precise location data (geolocation) when you use features like Attendance or Tour to track time or locations for activities.
  • Expense Information: When you use the Expense feature, we collect data you submit, such as amounts, descriptions, dates, and receipt images, which may require access to your device’s camera or photo library with your consent.
  • Task and Activity Data: We collect data related to tasks, travel plans, or other activities you log in the app to help you stay organized.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve SODA’s functionality, including:

  • Tracking time and attendance for your activities.
  • Processing and managing expense submissions.
  • Organizing and tracking travel plans or field visits.
  • Managing and monitoring tasks to help you stay productive.
  • Ensuring app security and providing user support.
  • Improving app performance and user experience.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. Your data is only shared as necessary to provide SODA’s services, such as with service providers who help us maintain the app (e.g., cloud storage or technical support). All data collected through SODA remains under our control and is protected as outlined in this policy.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your data is stored on secure servers with restricted access.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide SODA’s services or as required by applicable laws. When your data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights under applicable laws, such as the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email. Please review this policy periodically.
