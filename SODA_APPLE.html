Privacy Policy for SODA

Effective Date: July 15, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the SODA mobile application. This App is an enterprise solution provided by GISODA Co., Ltd. for the exclusive use of authorized employees and personnel of your business.

By using the SODA App, you acknowledge that your information will be collected and used in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

To provide the services required by the Company, the App collects information necessary for business operations. This includes:

  • Personal and Account Information: To create and manage your account, we collect information such as your name, employee ID, and other credentials provided by the Company.
  • Location Data: We collect precise location data (geolocation) when you use specific features of the App, such as Attendance and Tour management, to verify locations for work-related activities.
  • Expense Information: When you use the Expense feature, we collect data you submit, including amounts, descriptions, dates, and images of receipts which may require access to your device's camera or photo library with your permission.
  • Task and Activity Data: We collect information related to tasks assigned to you, tour reports, and other work-related activities you log in the App.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly for the business purposes of the Company. These purposes include:

  • To verify employee attendance at designated work sites.
  • To manage, process, and reimburse business-related expenses.
  • To plan, track, and create reports for official tours and field visits.
  • To assign, monitor, and manage work-related tasks and objectives.
  • To ensure the security of the App and to provide you with user support.
  • To provide the Company with data necessary for its business operations and management.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

As this App is an enterprise tool, all data collected through it is considered the property of you and us we will not be share it with other third party

We, the Developer (GISODA), do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties for their own marketing purposes. We only access data as necessary to maintain the App and provide technical support.

5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Data is stored on secure servers, and access is restricted to authorized personnel of the Developer and the Company.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as you are an active user of the App or as required by the Company's data retention policies and applicable laws. When data is no longer needed, it will be securely deleted according to the Company's instructions.

7. Your Rights

As an employee of the Company, you may have certain rights regarding your data under applicable law. These may include the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your data. To exercise these rights, please contact your designated representative at GISODA, who will coordinate with us as necessary.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify the Company of any material changes. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the App's data practices, please contact us first. For questions related to your data as an employee, please contact your employer.
